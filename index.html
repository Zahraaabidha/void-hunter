VOID HUNTER

Gesture-Controlled Space Fighter
🖐
Open Hand — Pilot
Your fighter tracks your open palm in real time. Move your hand anywhere on screen to steer.
✊
Fist — Fire Lasers
Clench your hand into a fist to fire twin lasers. Hold it down to keep shooting continuously.
✊✊
Both Fists — Missiles
Clench both fists at once to launch 4 homing missiles. Watch the cooldown ring before reloading.
Enemy Types
ScoutFast · 1 HP · 50×wave pts
CruiserSlow · 3 HP · Shoots back · 160×wave pts
Swarm PackWave 5+ · 6 per pack · Extremely fast
Combat Tips
Destroy all enemies in a wave to earn a score bonus.
You have 3 shield points — lost to collisions and enemy fire.
Missiles re-home to the nearest enemy if their target is destroyed.
Enemy speed increases every wave. Don’t let them reach you.
Chrome or Edge required · Webcam permission needed at launch · A tutorial will run before your first wave
